About Us

Building stronger communities through education and empowerment

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Skybenn Foundation began with a simple mission: to provide accessible education and mentorship to underserved communities in Hollywood, Florida. What started as a small tutoring program has grown into a comprehensive community development initiative.

Our founder, inspired by personal experiences with educational barriers, recognized the transformative power of mentorship and skill-building. Today, we serve over 500 community members annually through our various programs.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through partnerships with local businesses, schools, and volunteers, we continue to expand our impact.
